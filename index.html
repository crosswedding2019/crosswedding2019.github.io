Our Story

What started in High School stayed alive though the long distance and lead to this: the best moment in our lives

  • First We Meet

    July 2010

    Our Bride and Groom were formally introduced during an especially hot July Marching Band day on lunch break. While no fairy tale love at first sight, positive first impressions were made. In an activity where you spent 20+ hours a week during the summer and at least 15 hours during the fall, there was plenty of time to get to know each other.

  • Confession

    December 3, 2010

    It was the perfect setup: text asking to meet at his locker before class started and ask him out. Instead, Sidney handed her phone with the question prewritten because she was to nervous to form coherent sentences. Ryan thankfully accepted this with an awkward "sure" and Sidney bolted back to class before he could change his mind (or bother to see what he wanted to ask). Mission Accomplished.

  • Graduations

    August 2013

    After graduating in May of that year Ryan left for the University of Toledo for some higher education. The following year brought Sidney to Wright State University. With a 3 hour drive between them the relationship turned long distance. Countdowns to the next school break or long weekend visits kept things going. It was hard, but as the saying goes: distance only makes the heart grow fonder.

  • The Big Question

    December 3, 2017

    As December 3rd rolled around for the 7th time and the usually anniversary trail walking ended with an extra surpise. Ryan was nervous, Sidney was oblivious, and the box had a plastic dinosaur ring. Everything was perfect.

  • Graduations Part 2!

    August 25, 2017

    Summer of 2018 brought Ryan back Home, 2 degrees in hand and ready to jump into the workforce. The long distance was over and the real wedding planning finally kicked into high gear.
